About Me

I am a 3D Modeller and I use various software such as Blender, Adobe Substance 3D Painter, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Premiere Pro to create 3D models and artwork. I enjoy creating both stylized and realistic environments and am constantly striving to improve my skills in 3D modeling.
Also, I enjoy playing a variety of genres including Multiplayer, FPS, and Strategy games. In my free time, I love listening to all kinds of music and watching memes for a good laugh.
I am always on the lookout for new and exciting games to play and I enjoy spending my free time immersing myself in different virtual worlds.
